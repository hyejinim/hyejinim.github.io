Overview

Donare is a mobile application that helps people easily and delightfully donate items to causes they care about. I worked in a cross-functional team. As the main UX/UI designer, I was involved in all phases of the project, from mapping out the problem to delivering final designs.


Final Design


The Opportunity


User Side

• Often times, people have the heart to donate but are not aware of which charities are out there
• People do not have the time to actively seek charities out


Charity Side

• Charities often receive donations that are irrelevant/ unneeded for their cause
• Charity organizations need more donations

Evaluation

We conducted usability testing with the final version of prototype. User satisfaction improved on all metrics comparing to the first prototype.


Metrics:
• How easy did you find the donation process on a scale from 1 to 5 (5 easiest)?: 4.2 to 4.66 (+.46)
• How many times did you not know what to do next?: 2 to 0.66 (-1.34)
• How easy was it to use to achieve your goal? (5 easiest): 3.9 to 4.66 (+.76)
• Did you like the experience of using the app? (1 to 5): 3.8 to 4.33 (+.53)


Three More Features

Sharing donation needs with friends

Sometimes, those around you may need what you are trying to donate. With this feature, you could help those around you who you didn't know needed help.

Thank you messages from recipients

We as humans can only be so altruistic. A thank you from the charities / recipients of the donations would be very rewarding to the users, which would motivate them to utilize our service more.

Timeline news of liked charities or topics

By providing updates regarding the charities that the user has liked, the user can be continuously engaged, preventing the experience becoming a one-time use.
