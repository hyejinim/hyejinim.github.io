Donare: Donation to Your Cause

SEP 2017 - DEC 2017

OVERVIEW

Donare is a mobile application that helps people easily and delightfully donate irems to causes they care about.

I worked in a cross-functional team. As the main UX/UI designer, I was involved in all phases of the project, from mapping out the problem to delivering final designs.

RESPONSIBILITIES

• Concept development
• UX design
• Visual design
• Prototyping
• User testing

CONTEXT

CS171: Human-Computer Interaction, Tufts University
Team: Vivien Chen, Joseph, Kam Chan Kang, Behnam Heydarshahi

THE OPPORTUNITY

User Side

• Often times, people have the heart to donate but are not aware of which charities are out there
• People do not have the time to actively seek them out

Charity Side

• Charities often receive donations that are irrelevant/ unneeded for their cause
• Charity organizations need more donations

THREE MORE FEATURES

Sharing donation needs with friends

Sometimes, those around you may need what you are trying to donate. With this feature, you could help those around you who you didn't know needed help.

Thank you messages from recipients

We as humans can only be so altruistic. A thank you from the charities / recipients of the donations would be very rewarding to the users, which would motivate them to utilize our service more.

Timeline news of liked charities or topics

By providing updates regarding the charities that the user has liked, the user can be continuously engaged, preventing the experience becoming a one-time use.
