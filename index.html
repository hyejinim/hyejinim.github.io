Alarm Clock

#PRODUCTIVITY #BEHAVIORCHANGE

"How might we help the sleepyhead start their day at the planned time?"

I found that people sometimes fail to start their day at the planned time. One reason would be that they set an alarm at a miscalculated time. The other one is that people often habitually do snooze alarm clocks and delay their wake up time.

To solve this problem, this app provides information about how their decisions affect a day. While setting an alarm time, a user can check how much time he or she has in three categories of a day. By allowing the user to figure that out, the app helps the user set the ideal wake up time, prevent doing habitual snoozes, and motivate to start a day at the planned time.

Wakeup time is not just a moment but it affects the whole day. I would like to enhance the awareness of people who used to be not punctual by presenting important information. SEE MORE

Visualizing Nonlinear Narratives with Story Curves

#VISUALIZATION #NONLINEAR-NARRATIVE #VISUAL-PATTERNS

Nam Wook Kim, Benjamin Bach, Hyejin Im, Sasha Schriber, Markus Gross, Hanspeter Pfister
IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics, 2018, IEEE VIS (InfoVis)
Kantar Information is Beautiful Awards Longlist

Story curves visualize the nonlinear narrative of a movie by showing the order in which events are told in the movie and comparing them to their actual chronological order, resulting in possibly meandering visual patterns in the curve. We also developed Story Explorer, an interactive tool that visualizes a story curve together with complementary information such as characters and settings.

I am in charged with representing data and tagging data for visualization. SEE MORE
